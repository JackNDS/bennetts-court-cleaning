About

We restore every court like it is our own. Mitchell quotes every job, does every job and guarantees every job. Complete the form above now for a free quote.

I’m Mitchell Bennett. My family first entered the tennis court industry in 1965 when my great grand parents Louise and Jim built their own tennis centre with 8 loam courts and 1 grass court on Sydneys northern beaches. Since then our involvement in tennis has included everything from playing, coaching, building and renovating courts, and of course cleaning tennis courts. In 2004 my father Anthony undertook the task of inventing a new way to clean artificial grass tennis courts. He engaged with manufacturers of artificial grass, engineers, and colleagues who all helped create what became known as the “Big Red System” to clean artificial grass tennis courts. Big Red is the machine which houses the magic which when combined with several other processes forms the entire cleaning system. It could have a more scientific name but we are tennis court experts and not scientists, and we like Big Red. I did my first court clean at the age of 19. I had seen Big Red in action many times before, but had never fully understood the total system until I actually worked on the court with my father. In 2021 I created my own business which concentrates only on cleaning tennis courts. I refer my customers to the best people for all their other tennis court requirements (nets, fences, new surfaces, even balls, racquets, etc). I personally do every court clean. I often have an off sider (occasionally my father) and I always treat every court as if it is my own so I take great care and make sure I achieve the best possible outcome for you.
